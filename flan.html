Flan - The Golden Plate, The Royalty Amongs Puddings

When mentioning the word "pudding" the first sweet that would pop in one's mind is always the Flan, flagship of all puddings

The Flan is divided into 2 layers: the thick yet silky golden yolk and the bitter sweet caramel layer dripping from the top of the yolk. The combination of these two layers is a miracle in and of itself but missing either one of these will forever make a Flan incomplete.

Make one and try for yourself today!
